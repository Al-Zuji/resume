I'm Muhammad Syahmi — an engineering project manager who builds autonomous robots, the operator GUIs that drive them, and the IIoT platforms that monitor them. Seven years end-to-end: concept → prototype → field trial → production deployment.

Currently leading a cross-functional team of 6–7 engineers at Sailcott (M) International across software, electrical, and mechanical disciplines. I turn ambitious R&D into production-ready automation that ships on time, on budget, and built to scale. Targeting Engineering Manager roles where robotics architecture, hands-on team development, and product-driven automation strategy intersect.
